Hopes and Dreams

Why Galvanize

I finally decided to go to Galvanize because after trying to self teach for years there were still a LOT of things I didn't understand.
I am really excited to start this path that I have moved on and off of for so long. The thing that drove me to this line of work is my
passion for puzzles. Coding is like having a big puzzle that has differnt pieces that all fit in the same space and figuring out which
pieces work best for each new situation is so much fun, even if there is a lot of banging your head off a keyboard involved.

After Galvanize?

I haven't really given much thought to where I want to go after the program is finished. There are some social media outlets that might
be fun like Twitter or Pinterest. I was also looking at ways I might give back to the medical community that I was a part of for much of
my young adult life. I think before I really decide I would like to flex my coding muscles and see what I enjoy most, I guess we will see.
